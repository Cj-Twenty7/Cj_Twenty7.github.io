IRyS

The Vsinger from Hololive English
Project: Hope


IRyS is a virtual singer associated with Hololive as part of the EN (English) branch, Project: Hope. As a vsinger, she is much more focused in making music than most members.
Since debut, she has released a variety of EP's, singles and covers.
